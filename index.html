The Indigo Demesne

  • Ancient, historic house owned by a lord
  • The lady of the land never had children but always wanted them
  • She collected dolls instead
  • She met with witches to try to make her conceive with a child
  • The black magic warped the land and the lord grew sick and died
  • Instead of being able to bear children, her dolls came to life and were all twisted
  • The dolls killed the servants, requiring a massive graveyard to be built
  • The lady sunk into madness, believing her dolls were her children even as they did terrible things
  • Most of the lands of the house are now filled with graves, spiders, and a coven of witches that the lady dealt with
  • The house itself is haunted by the spirits of the deceased servants
