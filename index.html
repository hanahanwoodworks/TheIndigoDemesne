The Indigo Demesne

  • The lady of the land never had children but always wanted them
  • She collected dolls instead for several years
  • Her desperation to have children grew and drove her to make deals with a coven of witches
  • The black magic warped the land and the lord grew sick
  • Instead of being able to bear children, her dolls came to life and were twisted and evil
  • The lady sunk into madness and believed her dolls were her children even as they did terrible things
  • The lady had servants executed for the slightest faults requiring the graveyard to be constructed
